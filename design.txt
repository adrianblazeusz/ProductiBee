Title: Saver-your-time
Description: An application with several functions, blocking the application and websites for a certain period of time 
             (e.g. it will only be released on Fridays from 16 to 20) or forever. This feature will allow you to send an 
              email to the selected person with information and a randomly created password to unlock the selection.
              The second function is the work time widget, after clicking on the widget, it will start the stopwatch for 20 minutes, 
              after this time, this user can simply unblock or continue to work during this time, any selected application and website will be blocked.
